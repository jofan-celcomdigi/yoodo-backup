ROAM LIKE HOME FAQ
FAQ
How do I activate International Roaming on my mobile phone?

First, you’ll want to buy one of our roaming plans. Currently, we have the “Roam Like Home” service, available in 72 countries. Your roaming will be activated automatically once you start using any data, voice or SMS. Remember, without a roaming plan, you won’t be able to make or receive calls, use data or send SMS, but you can still receive SMS and access public WiFi hotspots. Make sure your APN settings are correct. Click here to check!

What roaming plans does Yoodo have?

We have Roam Like Home, which allows you to call, text and go online abroad with the same plan that you have in Malaysia. It’s currently available in 72 countries for a flat rate of RM5, RM10 or RM20 per day, depending on the country. Check via the app to see the rates for each country.

What is Roam Like Home?

Roam Like Home is an international roaming package that lets you enjoy the same local experience while you’re travelling out of Malaysia. For just RM5, RM10 or RM20/day, take your personal plan with you when you go abroad. You can even buy a booster as usual, if you run out of Data, Voice or SMS. Roam Like Home is currently available in 72 countries! Check in the app for details of each country and the rates.

What is the expiry of the Roam Like Home Pass?

The Roam Like Home Pass is available for 24, 72 ,168 OR 360 hours from the time of activation. For example, if you bought and activated a 1-day pass at 3.00PM on 12th December, it will expire at 3.00PM on 13th December.

How many roaming passes can I buy at once?

There is no need to buy another pass because your Roam Like Home pass gives you access to your domestic plan’s usual Data/Voice/SMS quota for 24, 72 ,168 or 360 hours.

Do you need to buy a Roam Like Home pass every day while you are on your trip?

Not anymore! You now have the choice to buy 1-day, 3-day, 7-day and 15-day passes. Check your app for more details.
