FAQ

What is 5G?

5G is the 5th generation of mobile networks. With this technology, you can expect a better online experience using your mobile internet with 5G connectivity.

What are the benefits of having 5G?

  • Faster speeds, so downloading and uploading large content or data will take much less time.
  • 1,000 times more connections than previous 4G connectivity, allowing you to connect to more devices without compromising speed.
  • 5 times quicker response time, making sending and receiving information more efficient.
  • 5 times more energy-efficient compared to earlier generations.

What do I need to do to access Yoodo’s 5G services?

To access 5G services from Yoodo, you need to subscribe to any high-speed data plan and select “5G Internet”.

5G Bonus Data Promo: Purchase a 40GB to 100GB high-speed data plan and add 5G Internet to enjoy FREE 25GB of 5G/4G data promo.

Note: To experience 5G, use a 5G-compatible device and use your device within 5G coverage areas. The unlimited base plan will not be applicable for 5G services.

Is 5G available for iOS devices?

Yes, it is now available. Please ensure that your iPhone model is 12 or later and updated to iOS 16.4.

Does the current Yoodo eSIM support 5G services?

Yes, the current eSIM supports 5G services.

Do I need to request for new SIM card to enjoy 5G services?

You can enjoy 5G services with your existing SIM card.

What if I don’t see the 5G icon on my phone?

If the 5G icon doesn’t appear automatically, you can set your phone manually to receive 5G coverage. First, make sure that you are within a 5G coverage area so you can have instant access to 5G. Then, follow the steps below:
Setting > Connection > Mobile Networks > Network Mode > Select 5G/LTE/3G/2G (Auto Connect)
