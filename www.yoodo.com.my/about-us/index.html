About Us

Our Brand Story

We challenge the status quo. We throw out the rules and make our own. Our mission: To create a service that lets people experience full control. To bring the ‘Wow’ factor back into the game. Giving people a voice, allowing them a choice. All while keeping everything clear and simple.

We break boundaries so that everyone can experience freedom. We are Yoodo.

Why We Exist

Changing the Rules of the Game

No longer will we be slaves to the telco giants with so many options but no real choices. We want to give our members what they really want – by putting their plan into their own hands.

Giving the Power Back to You

We know that you don’t like being told what to do. So we got rid of the pre-made plans, the contracts, and the fluff. So you have more control over your stuff. Now you can call the shots, because you do it better. Ready to take charge of your own plan?

Creating a Sharing Community

In the bigger scheme of things, we’d love to expand our reach all across different platforms and regions. To make a community of likeminded individuals who only want to help each other get the most out of their mobile plan. Come and say hi.
